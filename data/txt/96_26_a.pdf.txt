LETTERS OF

INTEREST TO BID

The City of St. Louis, Board of Public
Service, is soliciting Letters of Interest from
companies wishing to bid on the following
construction  project  at  Lambert-St.  Louis
International Airport®:
TERMINAL 
1  COPPER  ROOF
REPLACEMENT, LAMBERT-ST. LOUIS
INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT.  Work of this
contract  comprises  roof  reconstruction  of
domed structures at Terminal 1. Primary work
includes but is not limited to, removal of
existing copper roof material to expose existing
concrete deck. Demolition will include all
flashing, metal edges, and insulation, removal
of lightning air terminal units, including cabling.
Demolition will include abatement of known
asbestos material.

New work will include the installation
of  a  new  copper  roof  assembly.  This
installation will include all required flashing,
cleats,  insulation,  water  barriers  and
components to complete a fully warrantable
roof assembly. Work will additionally include
new roof drain assemblies with associated
drain  leader  modifications,  new  lightning

protection system, new fall protection system,
cleaning and painting of exposed concrete
surfaces, patching and repairs to the existing
concrete roof deck and associated precast
fascia panels.

Estimated  cost  of  this  construction
project is $5.9 M.  Performance and Payments
bonds, insurance requirements, compliance
with Mayor’s Executive Order #28 and #47
regarding  25%MBE  and  5%  WBE
participation,  and  Ordinance  #69427
Workforce Diversity Goals will be applicable
for this project.

Those  firms  submitting  Letters  of
Interest to the Board of Public Service will be
provided the project plans, specifications, and
bid documents by approximately September
27, 2013.  The exact date of bid opening for
the  project  will  be  provided  in  the  bid
documents,  but  it  will  be  approximately
October 15, 2013.  Mandatory pre-bid meeting
will  be  held  and  will  be  noted  in  the  bid
documents.

Letters  of  Interest  to  Bid  shall  be
submitted by September 20, 2013 to Bette
Behan,  Contract  Supervisor,  Board  of
Public Service, 1200 Market Street, Room
301 City Hall, St. Louis, MO  63103.

Committee Substitute As Amended

An Ordinance repealing Section One,
part 86.040 of Ordinance 56716, pertaining
to the opening of bids, codified as Section
5.58.040 of the Revised Code of the City of
St. Louis, and enacting a new provision on the
same subject matter which allows a local bidder
to match the lowest bid when the lowest bid
is from a non-local bidder; enacting a new
provision  on  the  same  subject  matter;
containing severability clause.

WHEREAS, local businesses which seek
to enter into contracts with the City of St.
Louis are at a competitive disadvantage with
businesses from other areas because of the
higher administrative costs of doing business
in the City;

WHEREAS,  the  City  of  St.  Louis
desires to encourage businesses to remain in
the City and to relocate to the City;

WHEREAS,  by  enacting  a  local
preference law that allows a local firm to match
the  lowest  bid  when  its  bid  is  within  2%
percent of the lowest bid, the City hopes to
encourage and stimulate local business.
